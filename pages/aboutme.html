About me

This is not for profit, it is only to share among all these archives for historical purposes.
Who I am?

My name is John, an argentinian guy who loves history and more specifically in the period of the twentieth century

Why did I make this page?

I made this page because many years ago (more than 15) I had found on the web, a page where users uploaded files on the second world war and there were thousands of photos, I loved it

And?... Don't you think there are many websites on this subject?

One day my computer broke and I lost that link, years passed and I could never find that web, and looking for a little more I saw that it had closed, so my intention here is to do more or less the same, I know that there are other pages with identical purposes.

What about pictures of many users in the world?

Here it is not about selling or promoting anything, if it is to promote history I think it never hurts a new web with references to this, since less and less people learn seriously about modern history..To their respective owners, their respective credits go of course, I just share.
